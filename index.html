About Me

Hi, you can call me kevspau, kespau, or sharpcdf! I enjoy making games with Haxe and various frameworks such as Ceramic. I also like making all sorts of apps with Nim and occasionally Python, including GUI and CLI apps. I've learned many different programming languages, although now I focus on Haxe and Nim for the majority of my projects. I also have experience with programming robots in C++ using wpilib, a library for programming robots made for the FIRST Robotics Competition. I love messing around and making bizarre/useless things just for the hell of it!


I would love to collaborate on games, you can contact me on my Mastodon or Discord, sharpcdf#1672!
